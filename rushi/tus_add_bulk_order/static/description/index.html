1. Add Bulk Products in SaleOrder.

Install the Add Bulk Products in SaleOrder module
Go to App & search Add Bulk Products in SaleOrder module and activate.

2. Product Configuration.

After the installation of Add Bulk Products in SaleOrder module
Navigate to the Products menu. You can either open an existing product or create a new one. In the product form, you'll find a section labeled Bulk Order. In the Bulk Order section, enter a unique name or number in model name for the product. This name or number will allow you to easily search and select the product when adding bulk products to your sales order.

3. Sale Order.

Once you have configured a product with model name
Navigate to the Sales Orders, Open an existing sales order or create a new one. In the sales order form, you will see the Add Bulk Products button displayed.

4. Add Bulk Products.

Once you click on Add Bulk Products button, You will be redirected to this interface, where you can view the Sales Order Reference Number at the top. This allows you to easily identify and manage products being added in bulk to the specific sales order.

5. Added Product.

In the Model Name selection, enter the product model name that you have write in the product. Once you select the product, all its details will be displayed, including the product photo, name, variants, available quantity, and price.
You can then specify the quantity you wish to order. After entering the quantity, the Total Quantity, Sales Price, and Total will automatically update based on the entered quantity and product price.
In that you will see Add to Cart and Delete options. By clicking Add to Cart, the specific product will be added to the sales order. If you have added multiple products, you can directly add them all by clicking the Add to Sale Order button.

6. Product Added to Sale Order.

You can now see that the product has been successfully added to the sales order.

What is the Bulk Product Addition feature?

The Bulk Product Addition feature allows you to quickly add multiple products, their variants, and quantities to a Sale Order at once. Instead of adding products individually, you can select them all in a few simple clicks, saving time and effort.

How do I access the Bulk Product Addition tool?

You can access this tool by clicking on the "Add Bulk Products" button within the Sale Order. This will open a wizard where you can select multiple products, their variants, and input the quantities.

Can I search for specific products within the Bulk Product Addition wizard?

Yes, the wizard includes a search feature that allows you to search for products by model name or code. The system will autofill the product details to speed up the selection process.

Does the Bulk Product Addition feature update stock quantities?

Yes, the system provides real-time stock updates for each product variant as you add them to the Sale Order, ensuring you have accurate information on availability.

Can I modify quantities for individual products in the bulk list?

Yes, you can customize the quantities for each product variant individually within the wizard. The Total Quantity, Sale Price and Total will be automatically updated based on the quantities you input.

What happens if a product variant is out of stock?

If a product variant is out of stock, the system will display this information clearly in the wizard, and your not able to make order of that product variant to the Sale Order.

Is the Bulk Product Addition tool easy to use?

Yes, the Bulk Product Addition tool is designed to be user-friendly, even for large orders. The interface is simple and intuitive, ensuring quick product selection and order creation.

Is this module compatible with Odoo Community and Enterprise editions?

Yes, it is compatible with both Community and Enterprise editions
