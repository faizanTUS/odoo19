Restrict Create Product, Product Variants & Customer

Take control with the Restrict Product, Product Variants & Customer, designed to streamline your processes and enhance data integrity. With this module, administrators can easily enforce restrictions on the creation of products, product variants and customer, ensuring that only authorized users can add new products, product variants and customer.

This added layer of control helps maintain consistency and reduces the risk of unauthorized or duplicate records. When the restriction is active, users will be prevented from creating new products, product variants and customer, ensuring that your system remains organized and secure.

Will this module prevent users from editing and deleting existing records?

No, the module only restricts the creation of new products, product variants and customer. Users will still be able to edit or update and delete existing records.

Does the "Create" button get removed only in Kanban view?

No, once you restrict the user, they will no longer be able to see the "Create" button in both the Kanban and List views.

Will this module work in Odoo 18 Community version?

Yes, it will work in both the Enterprise and Community versions of Odoo 16.

What happens if an unauthorized user tries to create a new record?

If an unauthorized user attempts to create a new product, product variant, or customer, they will be prevented from doing so. They will receive an error message or notification informing them that they do not have the required permissions.

Can I turn the restrictions on and off?

Yes, administrators can easily toggle the restrictions on or off depending on the needs of your organization. This gives you flexibility in managing the control settings as required.
